About

Photographer · Based in Bangkok & Shanghai

Lei Zhixin is a photographer whose work explores the quiet intersections between light, memory, and the everyday. Rooted in a documentary sensibility yet open to the lyrical, his images seek moments of stillness within the flow of urban life.

Born in 1990 in Shanghai, he studied visual communication at Tongji University. His practice spans street photography, portraiture, and long-term documentary projects. He is currently based between Bangkok and Shanghai.

His work has been featured in LensCulture, Urban China, and other publications. He is also the co-founder of a small independent photo zine collective.

雷志新是一位摄影师，作品探索光影、记忆与日常之间的安静交汇。他的影像根植于纪实摄影的敏感，同时向诗意的可能性敞开，在流动的都市生活中寻找静止的片刻。

1990年出生于上海，毕业于同济大学视觉传达专业。他的实践涵盖街头摄影、肖像及长期纪实项目，目前在曼谷和上海两地工作生活。

作品曾发表于LensCulture、《城市中国》等媒体。他也是一个小型独立摄影志群的联合发起人。
